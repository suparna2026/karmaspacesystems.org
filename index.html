About Us

Where precision meets purpose

Karma Space Systems is a systems engineering firm specializing in the design, analysis, and development of space platforms and new technology. We work at the intersection of cutting-edge engineering and real-world impact.

We bring rigorous engineering discipline to complex challenges — whether that's a satellite mission, an emerging propulsion technology, or a novel sensor system.

Our name reflects our belief: do great work, share it openly, and the universe responds in kind.
