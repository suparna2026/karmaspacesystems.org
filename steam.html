Our Programs

How we show up for students

We tailor our programs to the audience — from early curiosity builders for K–8, to rigorous engineering challenges for high schoolers and beyond.

🏫

Classroom Workshops

We bring space engineering into K–12 classrooms through hands-on workshops designed around real mission challenges — building, testing, iterating.

🧑‍🚀

Mentorship & Role Models

Our engineers volunteer as mentors, sharing career journeys and showing students from all backgrounds that space is for everyone.

🚀

Engineering Challenges

Structured design challenges — satellite design sprints, mission planning exercises, payload competitions — that make systems thinking tangible and fun.

🤝

Community Partnerships

We collaborate with schools, nonprofits, libraries, and institutions to deliver programming where it's needed most — particularly in underserved communities.

Ready to bring space into your classroom?

Whether you're a school, nonprofit, or organization looking for a partner — we'd love to connect and explore what's possible together.
